## What is Invoice Data Extraction?

Invoice data extraction is the automated process of capturing and structuring key information from invoice documents, transforming unstructured PDF files into organized, machine-readable data. This technology eliminates manual data entry and reduces processing errors by up to 95%.

**Key extracted fields include:**
- **Header Information**: Invoice numbers, dates, due dates, purchase orders
- **Vendor Details**: Company names, addresses, tax IDs, contact information  
- **Customer Information**: Billing addresses, shipping details, customer IDs
- **Line Items**: Product codes, descriptions, quantities, unit prices, line totals
- **Financial Data**: Subtotals, tax amounts, discounts, final totals
- **Payment Terms**: Due dates, payment methods, bank details
